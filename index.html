Cat Stages

Players guide a cat upward by landing each jump, naturally building coordination and focus with each run. The controls are straightforward, making it easy for younger players to pick up, while the challenge gradually increases to keep things engaging.

Along the way, players collect bits of tuna, discover new looks, and occasionally run into unexpected moments that keep each run feeling a little different. Some runs are short, others go further than expected.

The game stays centered on simple, skill-based play, with just enough variation to keep things interesting.

A light, fun experience that encourages attention, timing, and trying again. Designed to be simple, approachable, and enjoyable at any age.

Everything Your Cat Needs

Pounce & Combo Controls

Hold to charge, release to pounce. Swipe to move, cling to walls, and chain landings for combo multipliers.

9 Adorable Costumes

Dress up as an astronaut, rockstar, sushi cat, and more. Each costume gives unique gameplay perks.

Power-ups & Boosters

Grab boba shields, rocket boosts, and slow-mo clocks. Buy one-use boosters in the tuna shop.

Global Leaderboard

Compete for the top spot on the all-time leaderboard. Track your best height and fastest times.

Original Soundtrack

Zone-specific music tracks that switch in real-time as you climb through each unique environment.

Zen Mode

A calm, hazard-free experience with a soft light theme and meditative atmosphere. Unlocked at 100,000m.
